以deepin、树莓派、ubuntu为例
vim /etc/lightdm/lightdm.conf
把
#xserver-allow-tcp=false
改成
xserver-allow-tcp=true
然后
systemctl restart lightdm.service

以ubuntu为例
vim /etc/share/gdm/gdm.schames
找到
    <schema>
      <key>security/DisallowTCP</key>
      <signature>b</signature>
      <default>true</default>
    </schema>
改成
   <schema>
      <key>security/DisallowTCP</key>
      <signature>b</signature>
      <default>false</default>
    </schema>
然后
systemctl restart gdm.service

查看和配置x11的cookie
xauth list
例如返回
oyzh-PC/unix:0  MIT-MAGIC-COOKIE-1  4a8990913b52698fb02a0f666d70b075
然后把4a8990913b52698fb02a0f666d70b075填到 连接-> x11 > cookie选项，保存
